About Failure Is Fatal : Life Or Death

Failure Is Fatal : Life Or Death is an intense, scenario-based training simulator for nursing students. Experience the pressure of real medical emergencies where every decision matters. Master essential nursing concepts through high-stakes scenarios with timers, consequences, and realistic patient outcomes.

Features:

  • 3D CSS card flip animations for engaging study sessions
  • Multiple choice questions covering key nursing topics
  • Score tracking to monitor your progress
  • Image support for anatomy diagrams, medication images, and clinical scenarios
  • Drag and drop to load custom scenario sets
  • Category-based organization (Anatomy, Pharmacology, Med-Surg, Fundamentals, Pathophysiology)
  • Test numbering system for multiple exams per category
  • Death certificates with system timestamps for wrong answers
  • Sound effects and text-to-speech for immersive feedback

Perfect for NCLEX preparation, course exams, and clinical knowledge review. Experience the consequences of your decisions in a safe learning environment. All content is stored inline in the HTML file, making it easy to add new scenarios without touching any code.
